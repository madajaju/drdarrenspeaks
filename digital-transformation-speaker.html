Frequently Asked Questions

What are the four dimensions of digital transformation?

The four dimensions are People, Process, Policy, and Technology. Success requires addressing all four in concert, rather than focusing solely on technical implementation.

How does enterprise architecture support transformation?

Enterprise architecture provides the blueprint for transformation, ensuring that new technologies like AI are integrated into the organization's existing structure in a way that is scalable, secure, and aligned with mission goals.

What Leaders Leave With

Audiences leave with a clearer view of how people, process, policy, and technology must move together. The goal is not simply to modernize systems, but to create an operating model that can absorb AI, reduce friction, and support measurable transformation outcomes.
