Frequently Asked Questions

What are the four pillars of digital transformation?

The four pillars are People, Process, Policy, and Technology. Success requires addressing all four in concert, rather than focusing solely on technical implementation.

How does enterprise architecture support transformation?

Enterprise architecture provides the blueprint for transformation, ensuring that new technologies like AI are integrated into the organization's existing structure in a way that is scalable, secure, and aligned with mission goals.
