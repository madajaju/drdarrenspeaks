What to Expect

Darren's workshops are not passive lectures. They are facilitated working sessions designed to move leaders from "What is AI?" to "How do we run our organization with AI in a reliable, defensible way?"

Typical Workshop Flow:

  • Frame the business pressure, risk, and opportunity driving the engagement.
  • Assess current maturity across people, process, policy, and technology.
  • Align leaders or teams around the right operating model, controls, and adoption sequence.
  • Convert discussion into a concrete roadmap, decision memo, or implementation plan.

Delivery Options:

  • Onsite executive working sessions
  • Virtual briefings and multi-session workshops
  • Single-team cohorts or cross-functional leadership groups
  • Follow-on reinforcement sessions when implementation support is needed

Deliverables:

  • Customized Strategic Roadmap
  • Governance Framework Drafts
  • Workforce Training Recommendations
  • Infrastructure Requirements Documentation
  • Decision roles, workflow control points, or validation checklists where appropriate

Related planning paths: executive briefings and keynotes for top-line alignment, government AI speaking for mission-focused events, and higher education AI speaking for academic audiences.
