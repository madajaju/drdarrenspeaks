Book

AI-Augmented Teams

AI-Augmented Teams is for leaders and teams who want more than AI experiments. It introduces practical AAOS principles for combining human judgment, AI speed, validation, and accountability to deliver reliable, defensible outcomes.

Buy on Amazon Book a team workshop

How the book connects to AAOS

AI-Augmented Teams is a team-level entry point into AAOS. It shows how to define the work, keep human accountability intact, and add validation without slowing the team down.

Who this book is for

  • Executives
  • Team leaders
  • Knowledge workers
  • Public-sector leaders
  • AI transformation teams
  • Educators and workforce development leaders

Core ideas

These are the ideas buyers can carry into workshops and executive briefings.

Human judgment in the loop

AI should accelerate work, not remove accountability.

Decision packets

Track what was decided, why, and how the answer was validated.

Reducing correction load

Teams should spend less time cleaning up output and more time making decisions.

Use the book with an AAOS workshop

Pair the book with a facilitated session to translate ideas into operating practice and a 30/60/90-day plan.
