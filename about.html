About Dr. Darren

Chief Enterprise Architect, Professor, Author, and Executive Advisor.

Bridging Strategy and Execution

Dr. Darren Pulsipher is a seasoned technology leader with over 30 years of experience in enterprise architecture, cloud computing, and digital transformation. Currently serving as the Chief Enterprise Architect for Intel Public Sector, Darren is at the forefront of helping government agencies and large organizations navigate the complexities of AI adoption.

His work focuses on creating "AI-Augmented Organizations" where technology and human talent work in harmony to produce reliable, defensible results. Darren is known for his ability to translate complex technical concepts into actionable executive strategy.

Key Credentials:

  • Intel Public Sector: Chief Enterprise Architect, leading large-scale modernization efforts.
  • Vanderbilt University: Professor and adjunct instructor, shaping the next generation of tech leaders.
  • Patents: Holder of eight patents in cloud and grid computing.
  • Author: Author of AI-Augmented Teams and three legacy books on software development.
  • Podcast Host: Host of Embracing Digital Transformation and Embracing Digital This Week.
  • Open Group Leadership: Active leader in global technology standards.
