Long bio

Dr. Darren W. Pulsipher is an internationally recognized enterprise architect, technology strategist, author, educator, and keynote speaker whose work focuses on helping organizations become AI-augmented and digitally transformed. With more than 30 years of experience spanning government, higher education, and commercial industries, Darren has built a reputation for translating complex technology trends into practical strategies that drive measurable business results.

As a Chief Enterprise Architect, Darren advises organizations on artificial intelligence, enterprise architecture, cloud computing, cybersecurity, data strategy, edge computing, and digital transformation. His leadership experience includes serving as a Chief Information Officer, Director of Engineering, Chief Solution Architect, and Chief Enterprise Architect, giving him a unique perspective that bridges executive strategy with technical execution.

Darren is the author of AI-Augmented Teams: How Teams Deliver Reliable, Defensible Results with AI, a practical guide for organizations seeking to harness AI while maintaining accountability, governance, and trust. His work emphasizes the importance of combining human judgment with AI capabilities to create systems that can deliver decisions and outcomes at the speed and scale demanded by modern organizations.

He is also the host of the highly regarded Embracing Digital Transformation podcast, where he has interviewed hundreds of leaders, innovators, researchers, and practitioners from around the world. Through the podcast and his speaking engagements, Darren explores the technologies, leadership principles, and organizational practices shaping the future of business and government.

Beyond his industry work, Darren serves as an adjunct professor, teaching courses in cloud computing, scalable microservices, and emerging technologies. He is passionate about preparing the next generation of leaders and professionals to thrive in a world increasingly shaped by AI and automation.

A frequent keynote speaker and workshop facilitator, Darren has worked with organizations ranging from startups and universities to Fortune 500 companies and government agencies. His presentations are known for their practical insights, engaging storytelling, and actionable frameworks that help leaders move from strategy to execution.

Darren's mission is to help organizations deliver reliable, defensible decisions and outcomes at the speed of AI while empowering people to remain at the center of innovation. Through his speaking, teaching, writing, and consulting, he continues to shape the conversation around what it means to lead and succeed in an AI-augmented world.
