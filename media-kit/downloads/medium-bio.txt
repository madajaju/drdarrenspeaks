Dr. Darren W. Pulsipher is a globally recognized technology leader, author, educator, and keynote speaker specializing in artificial intelligence, digital transformation, enterprise architecture, and organizational modernization. As a Chief Enterprise Architect, Darren has spent more than three decades helping government agencies, higher education institutions, and commercial enterprises navigate complex technology transformations and achieve measurable business outcomes.

Darren is the author of AI-Augmented Teams: How Teams Deliver Reliable, Defensible Results with AI and host of the internationally recognized Embracing Digital Transformation podcast, where he interviews industry leaders, innovators, and practitioners from around the world. His work focuses on helping organizations combine human expertise with AI capabilities to improve decision quality, accelerate execution, and scale innovation responsibly.

Throughout his career, Darren has held executive leadership roles including CIO, Director of Engineering, Chief Solution Architect, and Chief Enterprise Architect. He is also an adjunct professor, researcher, and frequent conference speaker, known for translating emerging technologies into practical strategies that leaders can implement immediately.

His mission is simple: help organizations and individuals thrive in an AI-augmented future.
