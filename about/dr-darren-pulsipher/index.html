Meet Dr. Darren Pulsipher

Dr. Darren Pulsipher has spent more than 30 years helping organizations work through major technology shifts. His experience spans government, higher education, enterprise technology, and digital transformation, with leadership roles that include CIO, Director of Engineering, Chief Solution Architect, and Chief Enterprise Architect.

Today, Darren helps executives and leadership teams become AI-augmented without losing accountability, governance, or trust. He is not a generic AI futurist. He brings operating models, enterprise architecture, public-sector modernization experience, and practical execution discipline to every keynote, workshop, and advisory session.

That combination matters because most organizations do not need more AI hype. They need a clear path from experimentation to reliable, defensible execution. Darren helps audiences understand how to align people, process, policy, and technology so AI can improve decision quality and business outcomes at scale.

His speaking, writing, teaching, and podcast work all reinforce the same message: organizations can move faster with AI, but only if they keep humans responsible for the outcomes that matter.

For event organizers, that means Darren is a strong fit for keynote stages, executive workshops, leadership summits, AI strategy sessions, media interviews, and advisory conversations that need both credibility and practical next steps.
