Frequently Asked Questions

How can universities maintain academic integrity with AI?

By moving from detection to "Verification Discipline"—teaching students how to verify AI outputs and document their process of augmentation.

What is an AI-Augmented Faculty?

Faculty members who use AI to accelerate research, automate administrative tasks, and personalize learning materials for students.

How do you address workforce readiness?

We help universities align their curricula with the needs of AI-Augmented Organizations, ensuring graduates are ready to work alongside AI systems.
