Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI-Augmented Organization?

An AI-Augmented Organization is one that has integrated AI into its workflows and culture as a force multiplier, moving beyond simple automation to a state where human intelligence and artificial intelligence work in a defensible, reliable partnership.

What does "Verification Discipline" mean in AI?

Verification Discipline is the practice of systematically validating AI outputs to ensure they are accurate, reliable, and defensible. It is the countermeasure to AI hallucinations and the foundation of organizational trust.

How does Darren's role at Intel inform his speaking?

As Chief Enterprise Architect for Intel Public Sector, Darren sees the real-world challenges of AI adoption at a massive scale, from hardware constraints to governance requirements. He brings this "front lines" perspective to every stage.
