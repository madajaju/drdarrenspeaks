Future of Work in the Age of AI

A practical explanation of how AI changes knowledge work and workforce design.

Related keynoteBook Darren

Definition

The future of work in the age of AI is the shift toward AI-supported roles, workflows, and team structures that preserve human judgment.

Why it matters

Executives need to prepare people for augmentation, not simply automation.

Common challenges

The most common issues are fear, confusion, and the lack of role redesign.

Practical framework

Separate augmentation from automation, redesign roles, and teach teams how to work with AI responsibly.

Executive checklist

  • Identify impacted roles.
  • Separate augmentation from automation.
  • Train for verification.
  • Redesign work patterns.
  • Measure adoption and confidence.
