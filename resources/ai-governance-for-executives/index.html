AI Governance for Executives

Practical governance patterns for leadership teams.

Related keynoteBook Darren

Definition

AI governance for executives is the policy, decision, and validation structure that keeps AI aligned with mission, risk, and accountability.

Why it matters

Without governance, AI adoption becomes fragmented, risky, and hard to defend.

Common challenges

Leaders struggle when governance is treated as a later-phase task instead of the foundation.

Practical framework

Start with decision rights, identify risk tiers, define approval paths, and connect governance to the workflows people actually use.

Executive checklist

  • Name the owner.
  • Identify the risk tier.
  • Define approval paths.
  • Specify review and audit steps.
  • Keep policy connected to operations.
