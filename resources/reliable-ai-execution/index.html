Reliable AI Execution

Validation, workflow control points, and decision packets.

Related keynoteBook Darren

Definition

Reliable AI execution means AI-supported workflows are designed to be validated, repeatable, and defensible in practice.

Why it matters

Leaders cannot scale AI if the work cannot be trusted or repeated.

Common challenges

Typical challenges include hallucination, rework, hidden review loops, and poor handoff design.

Practical framework

Define the workflow, add validation steps, make decision packets explicit, and measure where AI adds value without creating risk.

Executive checklist

  • Map the workflow.
  • Define the validation step.
  • Name the reviewer.
  • Track rework.
  • Measure the result.
