AI Readiness Assessment

Questions leaders should answer before they scale AI.

Related workshopBook Darren

Definition

An AI readiness assessment is a structured review of people, process, data, governance, and technology readiness for AI adoption.

Why it matters

It helps leaders avoid expensive adoption mistakes and focus on the actual gaps that need attention.

Common challenges

Teams often underestimate data quality, governance, change management, and workflow readiness.

Practical framework

Evaluate readiness across leadership, data, operating model, and adoption capability, then prioritize the biggest gaps.

Executive checklist

  • Review leadership alignment.
  • Check data readiness.
  • Assess governance maturity.
  • Map workflow readiness.
  • Identify the first low-risk use cases.
