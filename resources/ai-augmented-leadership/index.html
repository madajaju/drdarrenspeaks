AI-Augmented Leadership

Leadership practices that preserve accountability and improve decision quality.

Related keynoteBook Darren

Definition

AI-augmented leadership is the practice of using AI to improve decision quality, speed, and consistency while preserving human accountability.

Why it matters

Executives need a way to talk about AI that is practical, defensible, and aligned with trust.

Common challenges

Common challenges include hype, unclear responsibility, and confusion between speed and quality.

Practical framework

Set the decision rights, define the validation steps, and make the leadership model visible to the team.

Executive checklist

  • Define decision rights.
  • Set validation rules.
  • Make accountability explicit.
  • Teach the team how to verify outputs.
  • Track where AI improves decisions.
