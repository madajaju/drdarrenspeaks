Public Sector AI Adoption

A pragmatic path for agencies that need AI adoption to stay mission-aligned.

Related keynoteBook Darren

Definition

Public sector AI adoption is the process of introducing AI in ways that respect mission, trust, compliance, and procurement realities.

Why it matters

Government and civic organizations need adoption patterns that are defensible and service-oriented.

Common challenges

The biggest obstacles are security, policy, procurement, and trust.

Practical framework

Align AI to mission, tier the risk, define governance, and build adoption around public value.

Executive checklist

  • Tie the use case to mission.
  • Review security requirements.
  • Define governance roles.
  • Prepare a policy path.
  • Measure service impact.
