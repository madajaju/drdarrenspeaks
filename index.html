The six stages of AAOS

Diagnose. Activate. Controls. Execute. Measure. Scale.

  1. 1. Diagnose - Identify workflows, decision points, risks, data readiness, workforce readiness, and governance gaps.
  2. 2. Activate - Build the organizational capability needed to support AI-Augmented work.
  3. 3. Controls - Define the operating disciplines and validation rules that make capability trustworthy.
  4. 4. Execute - Deploy AI-Augmented workflows with clear roles, responsibilities, governance, and measurement.
  5. 5. Measure - Track whether the capability is becoming reliable in the workflow, not just in training.
  6. 6. Scale - Expand proven patterns across teams, departments, and organizational functions.
