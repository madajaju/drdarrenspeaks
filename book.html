About the Book

In AI-Augmented Teams, Dr. Darren Pulsipher explores the practical application of AI in professional environments. The book moves beyond the hype of generative AI to provide a clear, architected approach to building teams that leverage AI as a reliable force multiplier.

Key Takeaways:

  • The Hybrid Workflow: How to blend human intelligence with AI capabilities.
  • Verification Discipline: Ensuring AI outputs meet professional standards.
  • Governance Frameworks: Managing AI use within your team or department.
  • Scaling Success: Moving from individual productivity to organizational impact.
