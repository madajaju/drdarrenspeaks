Frequently Asked Questions

How can we ensure AI adoption is secure?

We focus on "Verification Discipline" and secure infrastructure architecture (like Zero Trust) to protect mission data while leveraging AI capabilities.

What is an AI-Augmented Government Agency?

It's an agency that has moved beyond experimentation to operationalize AI across its workforce, using it to accelerate mission outcomes while maintaining human accountability.

How do you handle AI governance?

By implementing policy-based frameworks that define clear roles for people, process, policy, and technology in every AI-driven decision.
