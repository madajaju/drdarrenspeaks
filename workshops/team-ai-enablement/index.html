Team Enablement Sessions: AI-Augmented Teams

Teams move fastest when they have a shared way to use AI. This workshop helps managers and practitioners turn policy into repeatable work habits, practical prompts, and team-level operating discipline.

Book Darren Open Media Kit

What this workshop is for

This session is built for teams that need to move from scattered experimentation to standardized execution. Participants work through shared use cases, workflow patterns, and reviewable controls so AI becomes part of the operating rhythm instead of a side experiment.

  • Audience: Operations, delivery, and functional teams, up to 40 participants
  • Format: Two half-day applied sessions, onsite or virtual
  • Duration: 2 half-days per cohort

What participants get

  • Standardized AI-augmented team workflows
  • Reduced ad hoc and ungoverned AI usage
  • Team-specific workflow control points
  • Implementation and validation plan

Typical agenda

The team should leave with a shared workflow they can actually use.

1. Map the team workflow and identify the highest-value AI use cases.

2. Practice the working patterns that improve speed, quality, and consistency.

3. Define the controls, review steps, and handoffs that keep the work reliable.

4. Capture the implementation plan for the next 30 to 60 days.

When this workshop is the right fit

Use it when the organization needs team adoption, better consistency, and practical patterns the workgroup can repeat without ambiguity.

Related next step
