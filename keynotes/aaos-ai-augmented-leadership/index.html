AAOS: Becoming AI-Augmented

Becoming AI-Augmented is the disciplined shift from reactive AI use to a target posture where work is grounded, validated, and defensible. It builds AI fluency, domain depth, and Integrity Packet habits so individuals can move from AI User to Augmented Architect with reliable, accountable output.
