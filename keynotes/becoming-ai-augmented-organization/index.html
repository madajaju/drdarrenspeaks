Move from experimentation to an operating model that can scale.

Audience and outcomes

Audience: Executives, transformation leaders, government agencies, universities, enterprise teams.

This keynote shows how governance, workflows, adoption, and accountability connect when an organization moves from isolated AI experimentation to a reliable operating model.

Audience takeaways

  • Understand the path from AI experimentation to AI operating model.
  • Identify organizational friction points.
  • Learn how governance, workflows, and adoption connect.
  • Build a roadmap for responsible AI execution.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does an AI operating model include?

It includes governance, roles, workflows, standards, and adoption habits that make AI repeatable and defensible.

Why is this different from an AI tools talk?

It focuses on organization design and execution instead of product demos or hype.

What audience fits best?

This works best for executive and transformation audiences that need a shared operating picture.
