Generative AI Strategy for Executives

A direct path from opportunity and risk to business value.

Book This KeynoteView Workshop

Audience and outcomes

Audience: Executives, strategy teams, business unit leaders, innovation teams.

This keynote explains where generative AI creates value, what risk leaders need to control, and how to align use cases to business outcomes rather than chasing tools.

Audience takeaways

  • Understand generative AI opportunities and risks.
  • Build an executive AI strategy.
  • Align use cases to business value.
  • Avoid tool-first adoption traps.
