AI-Augmented Leadership Keynote

Helping leaders use AI without losing accountability or trust.

Book This Keynote View Workshops

Audience and outcomes

Audience: Executives, boards, senior leaders, university leaders, public-sector leaders.

This keynote defines AI-augmented leadership as the practice of using AI to improve decision quality, speed, and consistency while preserving human accountability.

Audience takeaways

  • Understand what AI-augmented leadership means.
  • Learn how leaders preserve accountability while increasing speed.
  • Identify where AI improves decision quality.
  • Understand how teams deliver reliable, defensible outcomes.

Duration options

  • 30 minutes
  • 45 minutes
  • 60 minutes
  • 90 minutes

Workshop extension: Extend this keynote into an executive workshop on AI governance, operating discipline, and team adoption.
