The Future of Work in the Age of AI

A human-centered view of how work changes when AI becomes part of the daily workflow.

Book This KeynoteView Workshop

Audience and outcomes

Audience: HR leaders, executives, universities, workforce boards, professional associations.

This keynote separates augmentation from automation and helps audiences understand how to prepare people, teams, and institutions for AI-supported roles.

Audience takeaways

  • Understand how AI changes knowledge work.
  • Separate augmentation from automation.
  • Prepare teams for AI-supported roles.
  • Build human-centered AI adoption patterns.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the big shift in work?

Who should book this keynote?

Does this include change management?

Yes. It is designed for audiences that need an adoption model, not just trend commentary.
